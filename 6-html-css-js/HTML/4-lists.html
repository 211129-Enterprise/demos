Homemade Hemp Milk

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of water
  • 1/4 cup of raw unshelled hemp seeds
  • 1-2 tsp of Maple Syrup
  • 1/4 tsp of salt

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender
    1. Plug blender in
    2. Turn blender on high
  2. Blend on high for about 2min
  3. Store in fridge for up to 5 days
    4. If you let it sit for more than 5 days, it will go bad
